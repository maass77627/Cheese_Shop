About The Owners

The owners are a group of many multi-taleneted friends. They all joined together wanting to create a spot in their neighborhood where people could come together and build community. They thought a charcuterie shop would be a great place for people to enjoy eachothers company as well as the many delicious treats available. In the group are farmers, gardeners, chefs etc and they love to use fresh self-grown produce in their ingredients.
